# CyberRisk AI Governance Suite

CyberRisk AI Governance Suite is an AI-powered prototype designed to automate technology risk assessments and governance activities across highly regulated enterprise environments.

## Overview

The prototype supports risk assessments across:

- End User Computing (EUC)
- Model EUCs
- Non-Model EUCs
- RPA / Automations
- Business Managed Technology (BMT)
- Generative AI Solutions

## Problem Statement

Large enterprises often perform thousands of technology risk assessments annually. These processes are frequently manual, inconsistent, document-heavy, and difficult to scale across business lines, risk teams, technology teams, compliance teams, and audit stakeholders.

CyberRisk AI Governance Suite demonstrates how AI can improve assessment consistency, accelerate governance workflows, and provide real-time executive visibility into enterprise technology risk.

## Key Features

- AI Risk Assessment Generator
- EUC, Model, Non-Model, RPA, BMT, and GenAI assessment modules
- Executive risk dashboard
- Risk distribution and control gap themes
- Asset-level risk register
- Control recommendations
- Framework mapping to NIST CSF, ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST AI RMF, and ISO 42001
- Executive-ready risk summaries

## Reference Architecture

1. Inputs: Asset inventory, EUC records, model inventory, automation registry, vendor data, assessment questionnaires, and control evidence.
2. AI Risk Engine: Applies risk scoring logic, identifies key drivers, and produces preliminary risk ratings.
3. Governance Library: Maps risks to NIST CSF, ISO 27001, SOC 2, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, and internal standards.
4. Workflow Layer: Supports remediation tracking, exception management, approvals, and human oversight.
5. Reporting Layer: Provides dashboards, executive summaries, audit packages, and board-level reporting.
